Site Plan for Idaho Hunting Adventures

Site Name

Site Name: Idaho Hunting Adventures

Reason: This name reflects the focus on providing information and resources for hunting adventures in Idaho, appealing to outdoor enthusiasts and hunters in the region.

Site Purpose

Site Purpose: Idaho Hunting Adventures Adventures aims to be a comprehensive guide for hunting in Idaho, offering information on hunting areas, regulations, resources, and a community hub for hunters.

Example Scenarios:

  • What are the best hunting areas for elk hunting in Idaho?
  • How can I obtain a hunting license in Idaho?

Color Schema

Main Color (#4CAF50)

Secondary Color (#333)

Typography

Typography: The website will primarily use the Roboto font for headings and Open Sans for body text. Roboto will be used for headings to provide a modern and clean look, while Open Sans will ensure readability in paragraphs and smaller text areas.
